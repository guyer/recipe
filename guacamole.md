Ingredients:
- 2 avocados
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips
- 1 tomato
- 1 lime
- 2 tsp salt
- jalapeño to taste
-1/2 cup cherry pie filling
  
Instructions:
- cut and mash avocados
- chop tomato
- chop onion
- chop jalapeño
- squeeze lime
- add salt
- and mix well
- add chocolate chips
- top with cherry pie filling
